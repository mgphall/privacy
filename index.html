Privacy Policy – All British Hills in the UK (BaggedIt)

Last updated: 22 January 2026

All British Hills in the UK (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require user accounts.

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Precise location
  • Contact details

All hill data, routes, and climb history are stored locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party service:

Google AdMob

Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect anonymised information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction data

This data is handled according to Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

The App does not receive or store this information.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

All user data is stored only on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app.

The developer has no ability to access or recover user data.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. It is suitable for general audiences.

5. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app changes. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
