# Privacy Policies

This directory contains privacy policies for various applications and services.

## Adding a New Privacy Policy

1. Copy `template.md` and rename it with a descriptive name
2. Fill in all bracketed sections with your specific information
3. Review and ensure all sections are complete and accurate
4. Update the "Last Updated" date
5. Commit and push your changes

## Naming Convention

Use descriptive filenames that clearly identify the app or service:
- `app-name-privacy-policy.md`
- `service-name-privacy-policy.md`

## Template Sections

The template includes the following standard sections:
- Introduction
- Information We Collect
- How We Use Your Information
- Information Sharing
- Data Retention
- Your Rights
- Security
- Children's Privacy
- Changes to This Policy
- Contact Information

Feel free to add additional sections as needed for your specific use case.
